Fridge Management System
The Fridge Management System is a platform designed to manage and monitor fridges provided to customers who purchase beverages from our clients.
Customers can browse available fridges, add them to their cart, upload proof of purchase (POP), report faults, and track maintenance.
Admins and technicians manage staff, faults, and client branches, ensuring smooth operation and timely support.

Features
For Customers
Automatic assignment of a fridge upon purchase.
Upload proof of purchase (POP) to activate fridge ownership.
Browse available fridges and add to cart.
Log faults if a fridge develops issues.
Track status of reported faults and repairs.

For Fault Technicians
Receive and manage reported fridge faults.
Track fault resolution and maintenance history.

For Admins
Manage staff members and their roles.
Oversee clients and their branches.
Monitor system usage and fridge assignments.

How It Works
Customer Purchase
Customers buy beverages from a client.
They automatically receive access to the system.
Customers upload a slip as proof of purchase (POP) to request fridge.
Customers can browse available fridges and add them to their cart
Once approved, the fridge is officially assigned to the customer.

Fault Reporting
If a fridge develops a fault, the customer logs it through the system.
Fault technicians receive and resolve the reported issues.

Admin Oversight

Admins manage staff, client companies, and branches.
They ensure smooth operations and timely support.

User Roles
Role	Permissions
Customer	Browse fridges, add to cart, upload POP, log faults
Fault Technician	View and resolve faults, update maintenance history
Admin	Manage staff, manage clients & branches, monitor fridges
Submit a maintenance/fault report after completing the work.
